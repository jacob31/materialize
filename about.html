About

Shalom! The name Khesed was chosen, because love is the corner stone for life. Elohim's(God's) khesed continues forever. He sent His son Yeshua (Jesus) to reveal His love to us. He did this by paying for our debt that has kept us from knowing Elohim. We hope you get a chance to know Yeshua's khesed for you.

Part of loving kindness is to love others as you love yourself. The key is to be able to love yourself first, before trying to love your others. We hope to be able to share things with you that can help you love yourself through living a healthier lifestyle. One of the golden rules in life is be able to love yourself and through that love you can start to love others with others.

Our research presented centers around wholistic health. We then share the things we find interesting information and products that cover a wide range that we believe to help the average person. If you have health problems or are taking medications, it is recommended that you look up potential interactions and if there are any- consult with your doctor. We are all for empowering you to be responsible for your own healthcare with the guidance of trained health specialists.

For example, our tea does not cause any more harm than a cup of coffee would but caffeine can decrease the effectiveness of supplements or medicines if taken shortly afterwards as it is a diuretic (causes one to urinate more) but it can also be healthy because it helps the body to detox.

Of greater interest are the effects of herbs on each individual. While we love the natural healing they bring, moderation is always key. Yes, even with tea.
